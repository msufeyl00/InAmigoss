About InAmigos

Creating meaningful and measurable social impact through collective action and digital outreach.

Our Story

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered NGO operating across India. Founded on September 23, 2020 by Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), we are committed to creating lasting change.

Our mission is creating meaningful and measurable social impact through collective action and digital outreach.

Our Initiatives
